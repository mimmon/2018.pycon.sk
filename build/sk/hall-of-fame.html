Sponzori

Ďakujeme všetkým sponzorom, bez ktorých by nebolo možné zorganizovať túto úžasnú konferenciu. Prečo sa stať sponzorom?

Diamant

Kiwi

Kiwi.com je online cestovná agentúra s prelomovou technológiou Virtual Interlining a zameraním sa na výnimočné služby zákazníkom. Náš kombinačný algoritmus umožňuje zákazníkom kombinovať lety aj s nespolupracujúcimi linkami do jedného itineráru, čo často vedie k významnému šetreniu nákladov.

Platina

Exponea

Platforma Exponea pomáha e-predajcom na 5 kontinentoch byť relevantnými pre svojich zákazníkov. Náš medzinárodný tím viac ako 140 profesionálov sa spolieha na silný backend tím vybavený nástrojmi ako Python, C++ a Go, Apache Kafka, MongoDB, Redis, ElasticSearch, Flask, RabbitMQ, Celery a ďalšími technológiami ako sú Docker, Kubernetes, gRPC, Sentry, Hadoop, a Apache Spark, vďaka ktorým pomáhajme našim klientom zbierať, analyzovať a reagovať na základe zákazníckych interakcií. A áno, prijímame nových členov tímu.

Python Software Foundation

The Python Software Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to advancing and promoting Python. The PSF supports and encourages Python events world-wide to advance its mission of growing the international community of Python programmers, and hosts the North American PyCon conference as its flagship annual event.

Gold

Kistler

The Kistler Group is a Swiss high-tech company and the global market leader in dynamic measurement technology. We develop and supply sensors, electronics and software backed up by a full range of services. In Bratislava we develop software for the next generation of IoT devices. With our software we help customer to manufacture higher quality products, to develop cleaner cars and to reduce congestions on the highways. Behind our success stand our passion for technology, our focus on innovation and a work environment that promotes teamwork. We develop vehicles that people can trust; we create measurement systems that save lives. Kistler helps to shape a better future.

Red Hat

Red Hat is the world's leading provider of open source software solutions, using a community-powered approach to reliable and high-performing cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies. As a connective hub in a global network of enterprises, partners, and open source communities, Red Hat helps create innovative technologies that liberate resources for growth and prepare customers for the future of IT.

Eset

Since 1987, ESET has been developing award-winning security software that now helps over 100 million users to Enjoy Safer Technology. Its broad security product portfolio covers all popular platforms and provides businesses and consumers around the world with the perfect balance of performance and proactive protection. The company has a global sales network covering 180 countries, and regional offices in Bratislava, San Diego, Singapore and Buenos Aires.

Solargis

Solargis is leading in advanced solar information technologies by developing new generation global solar and meteorological data and software that help solar power plants to become cheap and reliable source of energy. Today, the Solargis platform is used by more than thousand companies including world's largest energy enterprises and finance institutions such as The World Bank.

Silver

GitMate

GitMate is an automated issue triager. Based on machine learning algorithms GitMate labels issues, notifies relevant developers, identifies duplicates and maintains old issues. This reduces developers’ time spent on managing issues by at least 50%.

BBI International

BBI International is consulting company bridging the knowledge between business and IT. Our ultimate goal and burning desire is to achieve the extraordinary level of performance for our customers from the sectors of banking, insurance, automotive and telecommunication in Central European countries. Our most valuable competences are Applications Development, dInternet of Things (IoT), Data Warehousing (DWH) a Collaboration Management.

Slush Pool

Slush Pool is the world's first Bitcoin mining pool, which mined over one million bitcoins since its foundation in 2010. With back-end based mostly on Python technologies, Slush Pool helps to secure the Bitcoin network as well as stabilize miners' revenue.

The free and open-source PyCharm Community Edition is perfect for pure Python coding. PyCharm Professional Edition is designed for professional Python, Web, and Data analysis developers.
